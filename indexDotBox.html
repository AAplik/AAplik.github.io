How it works

When the program is run, the command line prompts the user to input two different color names to represent each player. Before the any moves are made, the board is blank. During each turn, the players can take turns clicking in between two dots to place their lines. When a player is the one to close a box, the box turns their color and they gain a point. Once all the boxes are won, the player with the most boxes wins and the game is over.
