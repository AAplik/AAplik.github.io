How it works

The game opens with some background dialogue scenes explaining that the main character, a little girl at camp, has been dared to go into the woods at night. Gameplay opens and we find ourselves in the middle of the woods at night with our backpack and flashlight. Using standard keyboard and mouse controlls, we wander through the woods eventually finding our missing badges as well as tools and clues. Internal dialogue from the main character is intermittently being displayed for the player.
